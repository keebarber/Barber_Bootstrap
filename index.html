5 Must Watch Episodes

If you've never watched the Office before, or if you have, 4 of these will make you laugh (at least once), and the 5th will be ever so painful to watch, but is a wonderful piece of television cinema.

Pure cringe. Michael promised a class of students he would pay for their college education if they graduate high school - and the day has come. I still can't make it through this episode in one-go. It's too awful. - S6 E12
